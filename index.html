About

A rapper turned programmer turned producer from Denver.

Bio

I grew up in Colorado with an insatiable love for music and improvisation. Over time, my curiousity led me to vocal effects processing in Max/MSP which snowballed into web development and creative coding in a variety of toolsets over the course of my career.

I've been enthralled with multi media installations for over a decade, exploring ways to tell stories in physical space with the latest tools and techniques.
