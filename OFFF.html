Campaign OFFF 20th Anniversary Short Film
Partners GMUNK, JOJX, The Mill

For the 20th anniversay of the beloved Barcelona design conference OFFF, close friend GMUNK directed an out of this world futuristic sacrifice where the last of the human race encounters the all powerful shimmer bots and the supreme.

The production involved building a series of creative technology installations with 4x Kuka KR150 robots with laser end effectors as the focal point. The endeavor was a cinematic exploration to orchestrate a far fetched stunning in that was captured by JOJX and composited by The Mill.
