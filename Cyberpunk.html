Campaign Samsung QLED launch with Microsoft and Cyberpunk 2077
Partners Stink Studios, Adam & Eve, Samsung, Microsoft, CD Projekt

Samsung QLEDecode was a part e-sports event, part CTF (Capture The Flag) and part real-world puzzle game. Streamers watched as five finalists compete against time and each other to virtually infiltrate a highly-secure yet interactive container housed the VTProDesign studio - with the hopes of unlocking the ultimate Cyberpunk 2077 Grand Prize.

Our team at VTPro custom modified a shipping container with tons of LED, motorized doors and gadgets, cold spark machines, a variety of displays and lighting fixtures, and finally a laser equipped KUKA KR 30 L16. From designing gameplay to organizing the stream, this project was a cross functional design and build that attracted nearly a million viewers on Twitch.
