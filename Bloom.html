The Art of Bloom

Team B1n4ry Visuals, Eric Davis, Megan Matthews
Project
  • Raining Flower Room
  • Custom LED, Projection, and Spatialized Sound
  • Custom Scents + Retail Experience
Campaign The Art of Bloom
Partners Intertrend

Myself and B1n4ry (Zac Layman) were asked to develop a custom hardware and software solution to create the lighting, interactivity and audio for a flower themed experience.

We used thermal sensors to track people based on their body temperature to illicit a sense of connection with the flowers, and your impact on them. Both the method of content creation and the display of that content represents the concept “As above, So below” to describe our cosmic dance through nature.
